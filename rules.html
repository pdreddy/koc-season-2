KOC Season 2 – TOURNAMENT RULES & FORMAT 🏆

📘 Complete Guidelines

⚙️
PLAYER MATCH LIMITS
👥
Doubles (Round-Robin only)
Max 3 days (6 matches). A specific Player1–Player2 pairing is also limited to 3 days.
🎾
Singles (Round-Robin only)
Max 3 matches.
📊
Overall (Round-Robin only)
Max 6 days total across Singles + Doubles.
⚠️ Violation: Zero points for that match. (Caps apply only to round-robin matches.)
🏁
COMPETITION RULES
👥
Team
7 players.
❗
Minimum Participation
Each player must play 3 matches or the team is ineligible for playoffs.
🩺
Injury Replacement (Committee)
Requires approval from captains & Uma.
🏆
SCORING
✅ Win: Win 3 out of 5 lines on a match day → 2 points.
❌ Loss: 0 points.
📊
Tiebreak (two teams level on points)
Points → Sets → Games → Head‑to‑Head.
📅
SCHEDULING
🕒
Match Days
Friday & Saturday.
🤝
Reschedule
Both captains must agree. Can be played any weekday before Fri/Sat schedule.
🚫
Change Deadline
No changes after the scheduled day.
📣
Score Reporting
If both teams fail to post scores before Sunday morning → 0 points for both. Winning captain must post scores.
⚠️ No‑show = Forfeit.
🧾
SPECIAL RULES & GUIDELINES
🏟️
Format
9 Teams • Round Robin.
🗓️
Duration
Oct 17 – Jan 31 (14 weeks).
🎾
Matches / Week
4 lines per week (2 Fri, 2 Sat).
🆓
Bye
One team per week.
🛑
Breaks
Nov 28–29 • Dec 26–27 • Jan 2–3.
📋
MATCH DAY PROTOCOL
⏰
Arrive 15 minutes early.
📄
Exchange lineups before play.
🚫
No lineup changes once started.
🎯
All lines must finish the same day.
🏥
INJURIES
📃
League (Round‑Robin)
Replace with a similar UTR player; requires Committee approval (captains & Uma).
🏅
Playoffs
Only if 2+ players are ruled out; requires Committee approval (captains & Uma).
⚡
NO‑AD SCORING
➡️
Deciding point at deuce.
🎯
Receiver chooses side (no 2‑point advantage).
🤝
CONDUCT & FAIR PLAY
🎾
Players make their own line calls.
⚖️
Disputes → Committee decision.
🙌
Respectful behavior is mandatory.
⚠️ Misconduct = Penalty.
